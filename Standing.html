About us

Boeung Ket FC (full name Boeung Ket Football Club) is a professional Cambodian football club based in Phnom Penh. The club was founded in 2008 and has since become one of the most successful football clubs in Cambodia. Boeung Ket FC has won multiple domestic league titles and has also participated in regional competitions such as the AFC Cup. The club is known for its strong fan base and commitment to developing local football talent in Cambodia.

Today the club is known for developing local talent, striving for domestic success, and contributing to the growth of football in the country.
